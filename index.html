Pindoo RSS

A free and simple RSS reader

Pindoo RSS is a free and simple RSS reader. It can help you subscribe to your favorite blogs and websites RSS and provide a comfortable reading experience for you.

Features:

  • Automatic reader view
  • Syncing via Inoreader
  • Dark theme for all supported iOS
  • Search multiple keywords
  • Built-in RSS link detector
  • Automatic cache-clearing
