Frequent Questions

How is privacy going to work?

Your data is still secure within Secspace. Secspace keeps your data completely local on your device and does not upload any data to any cloud service.
In addition, Secspace supports independent passcode and Touch ID to lock your journals.

How to lock my journal?

After you have set a passcode or Touch ID, simply just click the Home button to make Secspace enter background. Secspace needs to be unlocked with the passcode or Touch ID every time it is opened, even though it is just reopened from the background.

How to restore entries after I have deleted them?

Unfortunately, you can't restore any entry if you have already deleted them. Due to the privacy, Secspace only keeps one copy of your data on your device.
Think twice before you delete any entry. There is really no need to delete them. No one except you can access them. These text-based data is just too small to make any difference to your device storage.

How to insert images into my journal?

Well, you can't. Secspace is a journaling tool that focuses on the text. We encourage you to reflect on writing text.

Why do I need to set a passcode when setting up Touch ID?

When it comes to authentication, a passcode is necessary, and Touch ID is optional. Because Touch ID can be unavailable sometimes, like right after the shower. So a passcode is always a good guarantee for authentication.
